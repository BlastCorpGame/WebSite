Blast Corps

It’s a 2D action-survival game with a classic reimagined style, set in a world where enemies want to take control of an island. You take on the role of a lone soldier with a gun fighting off hordes of kidnappers, rescuing civilians and uncovering hidden secrets as you fight back. Survive, fight back and stop the enemy from taking over.
